Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of developers and tech enthusiasts. Over the years, it has grown into a global platform that attracts thousands of participants from all over the world.

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire innovation, share knowledge, and build a collaborative future for the technology industry. We are dedicated to providing a space where ideas can be exchanged freely and where attendees leave empowered and informed.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Amina Yusuf – A pioneer in ethical AI and one of our most inspiring keynote speakers in 2022.

Carlos Hernandez – Renowned developer and advocate for open-source technology, speaker in 2021.

Lina Patel – Global cybersecurity expert who led a masterclass in 2023 on data privacy.
